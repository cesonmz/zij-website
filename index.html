Who We Are

We are ZIJ-SATELLITES — a team of passionate students building the future of space technology.

Born from curiosity and love for science, we started our journey in 2025 with a goal: to launch Uzbekistan's first educational satellites and bring space closer to our peers.

After winning UzCanSat, we pushed forward with our most ambitious project yet — a full ThinSat mission. We believe knowledge should be shared, so we're building this platform to inspire others.

Our Projects

UzCanSat Victory

In 2025, we participated in Uzbekistan’s first CanSat competition and won with our custom-designed satellite. It was our first step into space innovation.

ThinSat Mission

Our current project is a real ThinSat mission — a small satellite that will collect real-time telemetry in space. We aim to launch it within the next 2 years.

Future Programs

We're preparing educational materials, outreach programs, and mini-sat kits to inspire future generations. Stay tuned for upcoming releases!
